Who We Are

Innovative Developers By Entrepreneurs Association is an organization that is entirely run by high school students. Our non-profit is made up of two sub-sections: the students teaching entrepreneurship and the students teaching computer aided design (CAD).


Living in the heart of Silicon Valley, there are many opportunities for business seminars, classes, and even conferences, but the main majority are geared for adults or cost an unreasonably high price to attend. Our program fosters business and entrepreneurship education through the Silicon Valley in the youth community. Along with that, we have recruited a team of excellent coders. Equipped with strong knowledge and a passionate team, we now have the opportunity to give back to our community by exposing middle school students to the business world along with helping coders understand where their passion crosses business application.
