iDEA

We believe that you have business potential. Our mission is to help you learn the basic fundamental skills of entrepreneurship and business. We are a registered non-profit founded in June 2016.


LEARN MORE







Fostering Entrepreneurship Education In The Community

We believe that you have the power to change the world. Within a few hours, learn how to innovate, and turn your dreams into reality. Given a few weeks, you will be able to learn the core fundamentals of entrepreneurship and business. You will be able to properly conduct market research, build a business plan, file a patent, and market your invention. Our mission is to prove that you have the ability to make the difference in this world with your unique and diverse mind set. We arrange workshops in local libraries to teach children and teens of all ages. This program is taught by high school students who have experience in the business field, whether it is starting a company, or being able to interact or work with one.

-Aneesh Goel, Founder and CEO
