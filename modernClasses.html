Entrepreneurship Program

One of iDEA's main programs is entpreneurship. This is taught by high school students who have experience in entrepreneurship or in the business industry. This class will help students learn how to launch a business in 4-6 classes with an idea that they want to turn into a reality. They will be taught key concepts such as market research, writing a business plan, generating capital through pitches, as well as marketing/salesmanship. Students will get the chance to construct a business plan with the help of their teachers and will present it to professionals during the final class.

Computer Aided Design Program (CAD)

The CAD program is a popular class to take for children who want to be able to build, engineer, and invent. This program has been developed by high school students who have experience in product development and CAD software such as Autodesk Inventor. The CAD course helps students learn the important skills in CAD, and its application in the business world. By the end of this course, the students will have built a product or object of their choice.
